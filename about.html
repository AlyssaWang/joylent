Welcome to Joylent!

This is a spoof of Soylent. No Soylent was harmed in the making of this site.

My name is Alyssa Wang, a first year student at Williams College in Williamstown, MA. I am a prospective Computer Science major and this is my 3rd collegiate hackathon.

This idea came about thanks to a conversation I had with Greg Szumel, where we wondered if one could live off of Soylent forever. From there, we began to wonder if it would be possible to never feel the need to interact with people ever again, and voila - the birth of Joylent: your alternative to human conversations!
